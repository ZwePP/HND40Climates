Carbon Footprint

The amount of greenhouse gases (like CO2) released into the atmosphere by a person is called their carbon footprint. When there are too many greenhouse gases, the earth gets hotter.

When we cut trees, we release that CO2 back into the air. Deforestation and the temperature rising too much is killing even more plants and animals. This creates a cycle of worsening the situation.

We need to stop this by using less fossil fuels which release carbon and planting trees.
