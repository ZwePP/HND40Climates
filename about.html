WHO WE ARE

We are a group of like-minded individuals concerned about the state of the earth and rise in temperature. There are many official websites which have documents about climate change but they are too difficult to understand. That is why we made this website to educate those who lack knowledge about climate change in a simple and easy-to-understand way.
